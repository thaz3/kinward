## Cross-Screen Review Questions

- Does every protected frame show the active Circle and, when relevant, Care Recipient?
- Are Circle-wide roles visibly separate from Care Recipient-specific roles?
- Are denied records absent rather than represented as empty or hidden placeholders?
- Are consequential actions paired with recent or fresh authentication where required?
- Does every destructive or authority-changing action explain the affected scope before confirmation?
- Can every status be understood without color?
- Does every interactive target meet Kinward's 48 × 48 CSS-pixel baseline, with visible focus, one clear primary action, screen-reader announcements, 200% reflow without primary-content horizontal scrolling, field-associated errors, and reduced-motion-safe loading?
- Do protected frames show active Circle and Care Recipient in text; use text labels for Pending, Active, Suspended, Revoked, Dormant, Denied, and Blocked; retain authorized input after validation errors; and avoid spinner-only loading?
